Dynamicland & me

The work on this page requires a little context. These research & art projects were all made at Dynamicland. The space uses cameras, projectors, and a custom language to facilitate turning the environment (walls, tables, anything you can stick a piece of paper onto) into a programmable space. For more detail I'd encourage you to read Omar Rizwan's project-based explanation of the space. He goes a little into Realtalk, the superset of Lua that Dynamicland is built with. It's a research lab started by Bret Victor, an intentional community, a gathering space, a learning space, and someday I'll write more about it. For now, here are some things I've made there in the context of research, education, art, and exploration:

  • Iambicland - a pun that turned into an interesting little experiment using small cubes to specify a number, specifically what Shakespeare sonnet you'd like to see.
